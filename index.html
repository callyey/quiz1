Local Food

If I could say what I miss the most from my hometown, it is the foods! The foods in Medan are so flavorful and diverse. Once you try it, you will never get enough of it! Here are some local foods you should try when you visit Medan:

Mi Rebus Medan

A noodle dish topped with bean sprouts, boiled eggs, and sweet potatoes served in a thick, savory gravy made from shrimp paste.

Mi Balap

A breakfast dish you can find on every street in Medan. Mi Balap is a stir-fried noodle cooked quickly over high heat, hence the name "Mi Balap", where "balap" means "race" reflecting the cooking process.

Tourist Places

These are some amazing places you can find other than malls in Medan!

Istana Maimun

Maimun Palace is a historic palace built in 1888 by the Sultan of Deli. The palace still has a living successor of the Sultan today.

Tjong A Fie Mansion

A stunning historic house in Medan built in 1895 by a wealthy Chinese merchant. It now serves as a museum showcasing the multicultural heritage of Medan.

Masjid Raya Al Mashun

A grand mosque in Medan, built in 1906 by the Sultan of Deli.
